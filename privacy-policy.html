Privacy Policy - Gmail Sidebar Cleaner

Effective date: March 22, 2026

Gmail Sidebar Cleaner does not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

What this extension accesses

  • The extension runs only on https://mail.google.com/*.
  • It reads Gmail page content only to perform user-triggered actions (for example, selecting similar emails, archiving, and unsubscribe clicks in the Gmail UI).

Data collection and storage

  • No data is sent to external servers.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs are used.
  • No account credentials, message bodies, or contact lists are transmitted outside your browser.

Data sharing

  • No personal data is sold.
  • No personal data is shared with third parties.

User control

  • All actions are initiated by explicit button clicks in the extension UI.
  • You can disable or remove the extension at any time from chrome://extensions.

Changes to this policy

If privacy behavior changes in future versions, this document and the Chrome Web Store listing will be updated before release.
